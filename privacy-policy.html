Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 16, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to FarmFusion! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and share your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding your personal data when you use our agricultural marketplace platform.

By registering and using FarmFusion, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Farmer Information

When you register as a Farmer, we collect:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • State and District (Location)
  • Crop details (name, quantity, price, images, description)

1.2 Buyer Information

When you register as a Buyer, we collect:

  • Full Name or Business Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Business Type

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

We may collect:

  • Device information
  • Browser type
  • IP address
  • Usage data and analytics

2. How Your Information Is Shared

⚠️ Important Notice for Farmers

When you list crops on FarmFusion, the following information becomes VISIBLE to all Buyers:

✅ Farmer's Full Name - Displayed on crop cards and contact details

✅ Farmer's Phone Number - Shown when Buyer clicks "Contact Farmer"

✅ Farmer's Location (State and District) - Displayed on all crop listings

✅ Crop Details - Images, prices, quantities, quality, harvest dates

By listing your crops as a Farmer, you explicitly consent to sharing:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your registered location (state/district)

This information is essential for enabling direct trade between Farmers and Buyers.

3. Why We Collect and Share This Information

3.1 To Facilitate Agricultural Trade

We share Farmer contact details with Buyers to:

  • Enable direct communication between Farmers and Buyers
  • Reduce post-harvest losses by connecting surplus crops with buyers quickly
  • Eliminate middlemen and improve farmer income
  • Build trust through transparent transactions

3.2 To Improve Our Services

We use information to:

  • Provide personalized crop recommendations to Buyers
  • Analyze platform usage and improve user experience
  • Send important updates and notifications
  • Ensure platform security

4. Information Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction:

  • Secure authentication using Firebase Authentication
  • Encrypted data storage in Firebase Firestore
  • Access controls to limit who can view your data
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 For Farmers

You have the right to:

  • ✅ View what information is displayed to Buyers
  • ✅ Update your contact information anytime
  • ✅ Delete your crop listings
  • ✅ Deactivate your account (contact support)
⚠️ Important: When you list crops, your name, phone number, and location become visible to all Buyers. If you're not comfortable with this, please do not list crops on our platform.

5.2 For Buyers

You have the right to:

  • ✅ Update your profile information
  • ✅ Control your interested crops preferences
  • ✅ Deactivate your account (contact support)

5.3 Data Deletion

To request deletion of your account and data, contact us at: support@farmfusion.dtl

6. Information Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. If you request account deletion:

  • Your profile will be removed within 30 days
  • Your crop listings will be immediately delisted
  • Backup copies may be retained for up to 90 days for legal compliance

7. Third-Party Services

FarmFusion uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) - Authentication, database, and hosting
  • Cloudinary - Image storage and optimization

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in
  • Remember your preferences
  • Analyze platform usage
  • Improve user experience

9. Children's Privacy

FarmFusion is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of FarmFusion after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Consent

By registering as a Farmer on FarmFusion, you explicitly consent to:

  • Displaying your full name on crop listings
  • Sharing your phone number with interested Buyers
  • Showing your location (state/district) on all crop cards

By registering as a Buyer on FarmFusion, you acknowledge that:

  • You will have access to Farmers' contact information when viewing crops
  • This information should only be used for legitimate agricultural trade purposes
