About FarmFusion

Addressing Zero Hunger (SDG 2) through a crop exchange platform that links small farmers directly with surplus buyers

Our Mission

Bridge the gap between surplus crop production and market demand, ensuring no harvest goes to waste

Current Focus

Our Platform Digital crop exchange connecting small farmers directly with verified buyers nationwide

Impact Goal

Eliminate post-harvest loss and improve farmer income by 40% through efficient matching

Platform Features

Current capabilities and upcoming enhancements

List & Manage Crops

Farmers can upload crop details with images, set prices, quantities, quality grades, and manage all listings from their dashboard

Direct Contact Access

Buyers can view farmer's name, phone number, location, and crop details to call directly and negotiate deals

Farmer Dashboard

Track total crop listings with real-time stats and manage all crops with easy edit and delete options from one place

Buyer Dashboard

Search crops by name, filter by category, location, quality, sort by price or quantity, and get personalized recommendations

Admin Control Panel

Complete platform management with user statistics, farmer and buyer accounts, product monitoring, and add/delete user capabilities

Live Market Prices Coming Soon

Real-time crop prices from local mandis help farmers set competitive rates and buyers compare prices for better deals

Logistics & Delivery Coming Soon

Integrated transport services to deliver crops from farm to buyer with real-time tracking and reliable shipping partners

Secure Transactions Coming Soon

Safe payment gateway with escrow protection and verified transactions for both farmers and buyers

AI Smart Matching Coming Soon

Smart algorithms will match farmers and buyers based on location, crop type, and market demand patterns for faster deals
